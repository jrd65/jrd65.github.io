Whiskers Learns Discipline

Whiskers, always playful and mischievous, would often knock things off shelves, knowing her owner would laugh it off. One day, after knocking over a vase, her owner calmly placed her in front of the mess and said, "Sometimes, a little self-control helps keep things peaceful." From then on, Whiskers learned to pause before leaping, discovering the quiet satisfaction that came with self-discipline and the joy of keeping her home tidy.
